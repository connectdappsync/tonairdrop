Q: What is TonTP?

TonTP is a decentralized cryptocurrency, the distribution of which is taking place through PoW-mechanism based on The Open Network Blockchain (TON).

Q: How much is fee?

TonTP charges some fees. They can be nominated in TON or in the issued tokens depending on the pool. Fees help to maintain service or to provide liquidity on the market.
